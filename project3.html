E-commerce Platform

Project Overview

StyleHub, an online fashion retailer, was experiencing high bounce rates and low conversion rates. They needed a complete redesign to improve their online sales performance.

Analysis

I analyzed their existing site performance, conducted user testing sessions, and identified key areas for improvement in the shopping experience.

Design Strategy

The new design focused on improving product discovery, simplifying the checkout process, and creating a more engaging visual experience that builds trust with customers.

Implementation

Working closely with the development team, I ensured the design was implemented with attention to performance and accessibility standards.

Impact

The redesign resulted in a 45% increase in conversion rate and 60% decrease in cart abandonment within three months of launch.

Project Details

  • Client: StyleHub
  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Role: Lead Designer
  • Year: 2024

Key Improvements

  • Product Navigation
  • Search Functionality
  • Checkout Process
  • Mobile Experience
  • Loading Performance
  • Trust Signals
