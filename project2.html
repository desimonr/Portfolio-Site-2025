Mobile App Interface

Designing an intuitive health tracking app that makes wellness accessible to everyone.

Mobile App Mockup

Project Overview

HealthTrack needed a mobile app that would help users monitor their daily health metrics while providing actionable insights to improve their wellness journey.

Research & Discovery

I conducted user interviews with 20 potential users to understand their current health tracking habits and pain points with existing solutions.

Design Process

The design process focused on creating a simple, intuitive interface that wouldn't overwhelm users with too much information. I used progressive disclosure to reveal more detailed information when needed.

Key Features

The app includes daily check-ins, progress tracking, personalized insights, and social features to help users stay motivated on their health journey.

Project Details

  • Client: HealthTrack
  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Role: UX/UI Designer
  • Year: 2024

Deliverables

  • Interactive Prototypes
