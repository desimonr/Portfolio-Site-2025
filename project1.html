Brand Identity Design

Creating a cohesive brand experience for TechFlow, a startup focused on workflow automation.

Brand Identity Showcase

Project Overview

TechFlow approached me to create a complete brand identity that would communicate their innovative approach to workflow automation while maintaining a professional and trustworthy appearance.

Challenge

The challenge was to create a brand that would appeal to both technical users and business decision-makers, balancing innovation with reliability.

Solution

I developed a modern, geometric logo system with a color palette that conveys both technology and growth. The brand guidelines ensure consistency across all touchpoints while maintaining flexibility for future expansion.

Results

The new brand identity helped TechFlow secure their Series A funding and increased their conversion rate by 34% within the first quarter after launch.

Project Details

  • Client: TechFlow
  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Role: Brand Designer
  • Year: 2024
